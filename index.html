Frequently Asked Questions

Where does my donation go?

Coopers Lane PTA is a registered charity (charity number 1017537) set up to raise funds for the school. You can find out more about what we do, and how you can get involved, on our page here.

How can I donate?

Coopers Lane PTA is processing monthly donations via Direct Debit. To sign up, click one of the buttons above. You will be taken to a form to enter your name, address and bank account details. Your first donation will be taken a few days later, and then at the same time every month.

Who or what is GoCardless?

GoCardless (link) is a service that allows companies and charities to take payments via Direct Debit. You will see "GoCardless" on your bank statement, but the donations - less a very small admin fee - are sent to Coopers Lane PTA.

What if I need to stop?

That's no problem - you can cancel your Direct Debit at any time, or contact via email here and we can stop your regular donation.
